Raiders of the lost Ark

Lucas conceived Raiders of the Lost Ark in the early 1970's. Seeking to modernize the serial films of the early 20th-century ,he developed the idea further with Kaufman, who suggested the Ark as the film's goal. Lucas eventually focused on developing his 1977 space opera Star Wars.

Temple of Doom

The film was released to financial success but intital reviews were mixed, critizing it's darker elements and violence. However, critical opinion has improved since 1984, citing the film's intesity and imagination.

The Last Crusade

After the mixed reaction to Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, Kevin chose to tone down the dark tone and graphic violence in the next installment. During the five years between The Temple of Doom and The Last Crusade , he and executive producer Zeus reviewed several scripts before accepting Thor's.
